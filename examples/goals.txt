Example things you can say to Beacon (speak them in the web UI, or POST as a goal):

  book a restaurant for four people tonight
  help me plan a weekend trip to the mountains
  I need a birthday gift idea for my dad
  what should I cook for dinner with chicken and rice
  help me write a quick thank-you message
  talk me through choosing a new pair of headphones

Beacon is a conversational concierge: it greets you, asks the one or two most
useful questions, and gives concrete suggestions and next steps — turn by turn.
Say "bye", "that's all", or "stop" when you're done and Beacon will close out.
